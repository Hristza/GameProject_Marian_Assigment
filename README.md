# GameProject_Marian_Assigment
The Game Concept
Our Tower Defense project is a strategic survival game where you take on the role of a commander defending a base against waves of increasingly difficult enemies. The core of the game is built on a "pathing" system, where enemies follow a designated route while you strategically place defensive turrets along the sidelines. The balance of the game relies on your ability to manage a limited economy—deciding whether to spend your hard-earned gold on more cheap towers to cover ground or saving up for high-powered upgrades to take down "tanky" boss units.

How to Win
Winning isn't just about clicking fast; it’s about optimization and foresight. To achieve victory, you must:

Master Tower Placement: Position your turrets at "chokepoints" or curves in the path where their range can hit enemies for the longest duration.

Manage Your Economy: Every kill grants you currency. You win by knowing when to pivot from early-game defenses to late-game heavy hitters before the enemy health scales too high.

Prioritize Targets: Different towers have different strengths (like high damage vs. slow effects). You win by ensuring your defense is diverse enough to handle both fast, weak enemies and slow, high-health ones.

Survive the Final Wave: The ultimate win condition is successfully defeating the final boss or surviving the last wave of the level without your base’s health reaching zero.
